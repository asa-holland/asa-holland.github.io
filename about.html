About Asa

I've been incredibly fortunate to spend the majority of my life in California, but have also lived brief stints in South Dakota and Utah. I moved to Irvine in Southern California during the fall of 2020 and the rise of Covid-19. I'm a Returned Peace Corps Volunteer (Senegal '17-'19), a UC Davis Aggie and a proud transfer student of California's Community College system. I'm currently pursuing a post-baccalaureate degree in Computer Science with Oregon State Univeristy. I live with my partner Ann and our adopted cat Pabbie. When I'm not at a computer, I'm out on a walk, playing board games, or trying to teach myself something new.
