Email Drafter

Problem: An environmental survey company referenced details on two proprietary websites to draft emails providing survey results to clients. A single email took between 5 to 20 minutes to draft.

Solution: Developed a tool to scrape survey details and draft preliminary emails in Word. Supported 26 common email types and reduced average draft time to 5 minutes.

Python: selenium, pandas, docx, pywin32
