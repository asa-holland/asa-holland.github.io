Preliminary Results Email Drafter

Problem: An environmental surveying company released emails to their clients with preliminary results. These emails were drafted by referencing multiple subpages of information on two proprietary websites. A single email took between 5 to 20 minutes to draft, depending on the complexity of the data needed.

Solution: Developed a tool to collect the information needed for any given email and restructure the data into a Word template document. The project included support for 26 of the most common types of emails being drafted, and reduced the average email draft time to 5 minutes.

Python: selenium, pandas, docx, pywin32
